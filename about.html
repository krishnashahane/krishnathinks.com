Hey, I'm Krishna

CS Student · Thinker · Builder

Currently pursuing CSIT at SSPU Pune — but real learning happens outside the classroom. I write about what I observe, experiment, and discover along the way.

My Story

I've never been a fan of rigid, formal education. I believe real learning comes from doing, experimenting, and observing how things actually work in the world. I prefer practicality over theory, and curiosity over repetition.

SSPU in Kivale, Pune is still growing, and that's what makes it exciting. It's not about status or brand — it's about how much you're willing to explore. I treat college as a lab, not a classroom. It's where I'm shaping my own version of success through awareness, action, and intelligence.

I don't want to be known as a topper. I want to be known as someone who thought differently and built something valuable from it.

What I'm Into

I'm deeply into technology, programming, and research — anything that sharpens logic and creativity. Outside of tech, I'm also a traveler, movie lover, foodie, and music addict who enjoys discovering new ideas and cultures.

Programming AI & ML Logic & Reasoning Books Music Travel Movies Food Deep Conversations Research Building Things Web Dev

What I Believe

Real wealth is freedom — freedom of mind, time, and purpose. I've learned that success isn't about applause; it's about consistency when no one's watching. I'd rather create quietly than impress loudly.

My only competition is who I was yesterday. This blog is a reflection of that journey — where I share thoughts, lessons, and experiences as I build, learn, and grow into the best version of myself.

"Think differently. Build intentionally. Live fully."
